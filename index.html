Free Online Image Merger — No Upload Required

ImgMerge lets you combine multiple photos and images into a single file directly in your browser. Choose from horizontal (side by side), vertical (stacked), or automatic grid layouts. Adjust the gap, padding and background color, then export as PNG, JPEG, or WEBP — all without uploading anything to a server.

  • No sign-up or account needed
  • Files never leave your device
  • Supports PNG, JPG, WEBP & GIF input
  • Drag to reorder images before merging
  • Adjustable gap, padding & background
  • Export at up to 200% scale
